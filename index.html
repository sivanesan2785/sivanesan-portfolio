About

Designing with purpose, clarity, and patience.

Hi, I am Sivanesan M, a UI/UX Designer from Tamil Nadu, India. I enjoy turning rough ideas into interfaces that feel simple, calm, and useful.

My process is grounded in curiosity, research, and iteration. I like understanding the user journey, identifying where people get stuck, and creating screens that support their next step clearly.

I am a fresher with a strong foundation in UI/UX principles, and I am actively building case studies to show how I think, test, and improve. I believe good design is honest, intentional, and human.
